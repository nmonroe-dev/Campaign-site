About Kevin Conner

Kevin Conner is the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of a renowned hospitality company, where he has spent over a decade managing top-tier resorts and delivering exceptional service to thousands of guests. His leadership experience in the industry has not only strengthened his skills in customer service and management but also provided him with the ability to address the diverse needs of the community.

As a community volunteer and active member of local organizations, Kevin is deeply invested in the issues that affect everyday Texans. Whether it’s working to improve customer experiences in his professional life or advocating for better services in the community, Kevin is dedicated to making a positive difference.

Kevin Conner believes that being a Tax Assessor-Collector is not just about numbers—it’s about serving the people. His understanding of local concerns and his experience in managing complex operations gives him the perspective needed to provide fairness, transparency, and accountability in the role of Tax Assessor-Collector.

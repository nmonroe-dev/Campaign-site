Fair Property Valuation

Transparency and Fairness in Property Assessments Kevin Conner is committed to ensuring that all property valuations are conducted with complete transparency and fairness. As part of this commitment, he plans to:

Implement Advanced Assessment Tools: Introduce modern assessment tools and technology to make property evaluations more accurate and fair, reducing the chance of human error or bias.

Annual Property Valuation Reviews: Ensure property valuations are regularly reviewed to reflect accurate market conditions and prevent outdated assessments from leading to unfair taxes.

Ensure Equitable Valuations for All: Work to prevent the overvaluation of lower-income neighborhoods or undervaluation of high-income areas to ensure that property taxes are fair and equitable for all residents.

Kevin Conner believes that fair property valuation is critical to building trust between taxpayers and the government. His plan will not only ensure transparency in the tax process but also provide homeowners with the tools they need to challenge valuations they believe to be unfair.

Enhanced Taxpayer Services

Conner is dedicated to providing an enhanced and user-friendly experience for all taxpayers. His approach to modernizing taxpayer services includes:

Faster Response Times: Implement a streamlined customer service system that ensures quicker response times to inquiries, enabling taxpayers to resolve issues more efficiently.

Real-Time Updates: Provide real-time notifications and updates on the status of tax payments, refunds, and valuation assessments, keeping taxpayers informed every step of the way.

Assistance for Senior Citizens and Low-Income Families: Offer specialized assistance to vulnerable groups such as senior citizens and low-income families, helping them navigate tax policies, understand their exemptions, and access available relief programs.

Kevin Conner's goal is to make the taxpayer experience more efficient, accessible, and transparent by leveraging technology and improving service delivery for everyone.

Tax Relief Programs

Expanding and Promoting Relief for Those in Need Kevin Conner is committed to ensuring that no resident is unfairly burdened by property taxes. His tax relief initiatives focus on providing meaningful support to those who need it the most, including:

Senior Citizens Relief: Offering targeted relief programs to help senior citizens reduce their tax burden, allowing them to remain in their homes and maintain their quality of life.

Veterans and Disabled Residents: Expanding property tax exemptions for veterans and disabled residents, recognizing their contributions and ensuring they receive the support they deserve.

Low-Income Family Assistance: Providing tailored tax relief options for low-income families, helping them navigate financial challenges while ensuring they are not disproportionately affected by rising property taxes.

Kevin Conner's tax relief programs are designed to promote fairness, equity, and compassion, ensuring that no one in the community is left behind.
